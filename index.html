Affordable Professional Computer

The large number of computer systems in use now leads to a large part of the work force in almost all industries being involved to some extent in the use of computers in the workplace. Thus, there is a need to teach people not only how to use the computer, but how to exploit it and to cope with the changes this brings.

History Of Infomation Management

The rapid changes in computer technology means that even computer professionals need regular training to keep up to date with technology; the new capabilities and enhancements. One of the problems in using computers relates to how the users interact with the computer; how they instruct the computers to perform the jobs that are required. At the primary level, there is a vocabulary of commands that the users give to the systems. At the next level, the system guides the users through a series of dialogues to perform certain tasks. Finally, there are the programming languages needed to instruct the computer to carry out its functions. In building systems for the users, developers often design with little regard for how the user actually would perform the task. This results in the need to the train the users on how to use the applications. On the other hand, this may indicate a need to train the developers on aspects of designing systems for the users.
